Into The Wilderness

Rendered mind needling me to walk
Into the wilderness,
Even though I have mine, I forgot to think;
Kindred heart swinging with me.

Everything's left; It's just my thoughts.
My coffin is empty.
The great glory of Ozymandias
Is ruined in vain; nothing besides remains.

Lucifer the morning star, rebelled against God;
His restless voice talks with the wilderness.
I thought I walked; my mind was dried.
Nevermind, but it never fills my mind.

Oh, poor mind of men!
